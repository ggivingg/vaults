### Key Components of a shoe
* the shoe last
* insoles
	* high heel - shanked insole board
* insold cover textile
* heels 
* upper textile 
* upper lining
* stiffiners (sit between upper textile and upper lining and shape shoe )
* soles
* insole sock 

### Key shoe tools 
 * water based non-toxic glue
 *  solvent glue (to attach soles and for tricky materials)
 *  plastic spatula for spreading glue 
 * silver pen
 * rubber crepe 
 * fabric tape measure
 * awl
 * masking tape
 * folding hammer
 * safety beveler 
 * heavy duty scissors
 *  craft knife
 * sandpaper
 *  heat gun
 * drill (for high heels)
 * dremel for sanding
 * sewing machine
 * thread (polyester or nylon)
