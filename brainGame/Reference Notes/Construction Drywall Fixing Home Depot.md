
## Tools 
* drywall
* oscillating multitool or drywall cutter
* blade
* drywall screws
* stud finder
* measuring tape
* straight edge
* pencil
* sand paper
* dry wall compound 

1. mark the studs 
2. outline the cutout
	1. overlap the studs on both sides
3. cutout
4. measure the new drywall insert 
5. cut new piece of drywall to size using oscillating tool
6. sand piece
7. fasten piece of drywall into studs with drywall screws 
8. cover seams with mesh tape
9. cover it all with joint compound
