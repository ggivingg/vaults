
# How to Defend Everything Paines 

## Volume 01

5 points of attack
* behind the neck
* behind the armpits
* behind the knees 

If you don't reach out you cant get caught in joint attacks

### Positions

#### Running Man 

* Two fingers worth of your elbow are exposed but the rest is jammed into your hip 
* Forehead on mat
* over the top of the chest 

#### Turtle
* Hands down towards hits
* weight on top of head 
* elbows jammed into hips
	* elbows slightly exposed a fingers worth 
* on toes 

#### Shrimp
* shrimping is a forward facing running man

#### Transitions
* the key to all this working is being able to transition between each of these positions 
* the higher the level of your partner the more important the quality of your transitions will be
